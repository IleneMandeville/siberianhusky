The Siberian Husky

The Puppy Years

Some basic Information

The puppy years for a Siberian Husky would probably
be the hardest years for the owner. The Siberian Husky
can not be left alone or else it will wine and howl and
be problamatic. This kind of dog is naturally hard to
train and owners may need professional help. At such
a young age it is important to start teaching the puppy
to sit still when being brushed, potty trained, and so on.
